About This Project

During my "Intro to Computational Media 2700" class, I had the exciting opportunity to collaborate with a partner and create a captivating 3D virtual cabin experience using HTML and A-Frame on the Glitch platform. Together, we designed and built an immersive virtual environment that showcased our creative and technical skills. This project not only allowed us to explore the possibilities of interactive 3D design but also provided a unique learning experience that was both challenging and rewarding.
