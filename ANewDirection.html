A New Direction - Ai Powered Game

About This Project

Teaming up with a partner for our class project, we embarked on a creative journey to develop 'A New Direction,' an interactive game inspired by the popular game BitLife. Leveraging Node.js, AI-powered ChatGPT, and various other skills such as CSS, JavaScript, and HTML, we crafted an engaging narrative-driven experience. In 'A New Direction,' players make choices that influence their character's life journey, with ChatGPT dynamically generating personalized stories based on those choices. This project pushed the boundaries of storytelling and AI, resulting in a captivating and unique gaming experience.
